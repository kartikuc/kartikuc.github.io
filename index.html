About Me

I am a final-year Dual Degree student in Electrical Engineering at IIT Bombay, specializing in Solid State Devices with a minor in Computer Science. My research interests include nanoelectronics, semiconductor physics, and device technology. Through various academic projects, I have explored different aspects of these areas and developed a strong foundation in both theory and experimentation. I believe in learning through practical application and have promoted this mindset as a co-founder of the HackerFab IIT Bombay chapter. I am driven by the immense scope for discovery and innovation in this field, which continues to inspire my academic and research pursuits.

Outside academics, I enjoy playing hockey, drumming, and tinkering around in Linux. I am also passionate about photography — you can find some of my favorite shots here.
